Yoga Offerings

Each style invites you into a different experience of presence and breath. All practices are adapted to honor your body's unique needs and abilities.

Hatha Yoga

Focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breathing exercises (pranayama).

Vinyasa Yoga

Flowing movements that synchronize with the breath.

Iyengar Yoga

Emphasizes precise alignment and the use of props.

Yin Yoga

Holds poses for extended periods, focusing on connective tissues and fascia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior yoga experience? Absolutely not! All levels are welcome. If you have a disability or medical condition, I can modify using chairs, props, or alternate positions. Each session is truly adapted for your experience and goals.
What does each session include? Every 90-minute session includes guided postures, breathwork, relaxation, and modifications to suit your body. Props like blocks, bolsters, and straps are provided.
How long until I notice results? Results vary per person, but most students notice improvements in flexibility, strength, and mindfulness within a few weeks of regular practice. Each month includes eight private, 90-minute sessions tailored to you.
